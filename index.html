What we do

Hardware–software systems for controlled physical experimentation.

Chora Systems develops modular engineering platforms focused on small-scale energy harvesting, mechanical experimentation, and deterministic system design.

We create controlled, auditable environments that allow engineers and developers to test, measure, and refine physical systems with greater confidence than ad-hoc prototypes typically allow.

Our approach

Reproducibility over improvisation.

Most early physical prototypes are difficult to reproduce and even harder to properly verify. Chora Systems focuses on platforms that prioritise control, instrumentation, and repeatability — so testing and iteration can happen with greater clarity.
